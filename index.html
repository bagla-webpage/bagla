Our Infrastructure

We have over 20,000 sq.ft. of warehousing space in the Mominpur – Hyde Road Industrial area with round-the-clock accessibility to Vidyasagar Setu connecting to NH2 (Delhi), NH6 (Mumbai), and NH5 (Chennai).

All facilities are secure with boundary walls, 24/7 security and CCTV, deep tube-well water supply, and generator backup. Our locations are equipped with broadband internet, telephones, fax, and computers.

Our fleet includes Light and Medium Commercial Vehicles up to 4 metric tons capacity. We handle last-mile delivery and outstation dispatches effectively.

Our 1000 sq.ft. office in South Calcutta is fully equipped and our staff is trained in SAP and local languages to ensure seamless operations.
